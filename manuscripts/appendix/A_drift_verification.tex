
\section{Drift Verification}
\label{sec:appendix_drift}

The Foster--Lyapunov drift inequality for UAS (Theorem~\ref{thm:uas}),
\[
\gen \lyap_{\mathrm{UAS}}(\Qstate) \leq -\varepsilon_{\mathrm{UAS}} |\Qstate|_1 + R_{\mathrm{UAS}},
\]
was numerically verified by exactly computing the generator action at each sampled state and comparing against the theoretical upper bound.

\subsection{Configuration}
$\Nservers = 10$, $\rho = 0.80$, $\temp = 10.0$, 20{,}001 states sampled.
Computed constants: $\varepsilon_{\mathrm{UAS}} = 0.28$, $R_{\mathrm{UAS}} = 38.39$.

\subsection{Result}
\textbf{Zero bound violations} across all 20{,}001 sampled states.
The drift becomes strictly negative for $|\Qstate|_1 > R/\varepsilon \approx 137$, consistent with Theorem~\ref{thm:uas}.
