
\section{Engine-Consistency Reruns}
\label{sec:appendix_engine}

During the experimental campaign, some early evidence was produced using a mixed NumPy/JAX evaluation path.
To ensure all publication artifacts derive from a single consistent engine, the \texttt{stats}, \texttt{generalize}, and \texttt{critical} experiments were rerun using the canonical NumPy SSA~\cite{asmussen2003applied}.

\subsection{Stats Rerun}

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\small

\begin{tabular}{@{\hspace{6pt}}l @{\hspace{20pt}} r@{\hspace{6pt}}}
\toprule
\textbf{Metric} & \textbf{Change} \\
\midrule
Baseline mean shift        & $+0.0007$ \\
Neural mean shift          & $0.0$ \\
Improvement \%             & Unchanged \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\textbf{Verdict:} The strong statistical win ($p < 10^{-66}$, $|d| = 26.3$) survives with no material change.
