\label{sec:appendix_stress}

This appendix provides extended tables for the stress test and alpha stability sweep.

\subsection{Stress Test: Scalability}

The stress test evaluates UAS under extreme operating conditions to characterize its behavioral envelope~\cite{meyn2012markov}.

\subsubsection{Massive-$\Nservers$ Scaling}

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\small
\caption{Massive-$\Nservers$ stress test: UAS queue length scales linearly with server count; Gini remains stable at ${\sim}0.031$.}
\label{tab:stress_massive}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.25}
\begin{tabular}{@{\hspace{6pt}}r @{\hspace{18pt}} r @{\hspace{18pt}} r @{\hspace{18pt}} r@{\hspace{6pt}}}
\toprule
$\Nservers$ & \textbf{Mean $\sum_i\Qlen{i}$} & \textbf{Gini} & \textbf{Per-server} \\
\midrule
8     & 14.30    & 0.026 & 1.79 \\
32    & 52.04    & 0.031 & 1.63 \\

\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

Per-server queue length saturates at approximately $1.60$ for $\Nservers \geq 128$, confirming linear scaling.
The Gini coefficient remains below $0.032$ at all tested scales, indicating highly uniform load distribution.

\subsection{Alpha Stability Sweep}

The sweep evaluates UAS across a grid of temperatures $\temp \in \{0.01, \ldots, 100\}$ and load factors $\rho \in \{0.5, 0.7\}$, confirming stable empirical behavior for all $\temp > 0$ consistent with Theorem~\ref{thm:uas}.
The main-text visualization (Figure~\ref{fig:alpha_main}) shows performance saturation around $\temp = 20$--$50$.
